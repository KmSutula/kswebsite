Pomodoro Timer App
(Project)

A single page application that is based on the Pomodoro technique of splitting up work sessions with small breaks. Adjust the time you want to spend working and the amount of time you want to break for, then press the play button to start the work interval! Also includes a pause button and a reset button. A alert sound plays at the end of each timed session. An effective way to study, and I use it myself!
